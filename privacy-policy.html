Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

At Recipes Maker, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Content: Recipes, ingredients, instructions, and other information you upload.
  • Usage Data: Interactions such as views, likes, saved recipes, and time spent in the app.
  • Device Data: Non-personal technical details like device type, OS version, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display and share your recipes with the Recipes Maker community.
  • To personalize and enhance the app experience.
  • To analyze feature usage and improve overall performance.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for third-party marketing.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

  • All payments and subscriptions are securely processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • We do not collect or store any payment or credit card details on our servers.

4. Data Security

  • We implement standard security practices such as encrypted transmissions and secure APIs to protect your data.
  • Despite our efforts, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. Use the app at your discretion.

5. Your Rights

  • You can manage or delete your uploaded content through your device or in-app settings.
  • You may request the deletion of your data by contacting our support team.
  • You may opt out of analytics and tracking via your device’s privacy settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that a child has submitted personal data, we will promptly delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase) solely for app performance insights. These services follow their own privacy policies, and we ensure no personally identifiable information is shared with them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Major changes will be communicated within the app or on our website.
